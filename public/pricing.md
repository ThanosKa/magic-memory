# Pricing — Magic Memory

AI-powered photo restoration using GFPGAN. No subscription — one-time credit packages that never expire.

Last updated: 2026-04-18
Currency: EUR (Euro). Your bank converts to local currency at purchase.
Payment processor: Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1).

## Free Daily

- Price: €0 / day
- Credits: 1 restoration per day (resets at midnight UTC)
- Credit card required: No
- Expiration: Unused daily credits do not roll over
- Best for: Trying the product, restoring a single important photo

## Starter Plan

- Price: €9.99 one-time
- Credits: 30 restorations
- Per-restoration cost: €0.33
- Expiration: Never
- Best for: A small batch of family photos (e.g., one shoebox of old prints)

## Growth Plan

- Price: €19.99 one-time
- Credits: 120 restorations
- Per-restoration cost: €0.17
- Expiration: Never
- Best for: A full photo album, a decade of school photos, or a multi-generation family archive

## Premium Plan

- Price: €29.99 one-time
- Credits: 350 restorations
- Per-restoration cost: €0.09 (lowest per-photo rate)
- Expiration: Never
- Best for: Photographers, genealogists, anyone digitizing a long archive or running client projects

## Policies

- Refunds: Full or prorated refunds on unused credits within 30 days of purchase
- Failed restorations: Credits are not deducted if a restoration fails
- Data: Photos are processed on secure servers and not stored after your session ends
- AI model: GFPGAN (Generative Facial Prior GAN), published at CVPR 2021 by Tencent ARC

- Magic Memory Premium: €29.99 one-time, no recurring charge
